Inventory Management

From simple tracking to full control

Manage stock and unlock advanced inventory tools as your business grows — so you never miss a sale or overspend on supplies.

  Watch In Action

Essential inventory features to keep your business on track

Vemli POS comes with inventory tools to help you track items, manage components and keep your stock under control.

  • Keep an eye on your products and their components, so you always know what’s in stock.
  • Get instant alerts when items run low or out of stock, helping you restock on time and avoid missed sales.
  • Keep your inventory organized by grouping variants — such as sizes, colors or materials — under a single item.
  • Create combo items or recipes from multiple components with stock automatically deducted when they’re sold.
  • Save time by adding hundreds of items at once using simple CSV spreadsheets.

Advanced inventory tools designed for growing businesses

When your business needs more precision and control, upgrade to Advanced Inventory, a paid add-on with tools to manage purchase orders, transfers, stock adjustments, and gain deeper insights into your inventory.

  • Purchase orders
    Create and send purchase orders directly to suppliers with smart autofill options that speed up reordering.
  • Transfer orders
    Move stock across locations with clear tracking of what’s sent and received, eliminating guesswork.
  • Stock adjustments
    Update stock levels with tracked reasons like damage, loss or corrections — keeping records auditable.
  • Inventory counts
    Reconcile expected and actual stock with full or partial counts to identify and resolve discrepancies.
  • Production
    Track produced items and automatically deduct components to keep inventory accurate.
  • Inventory history
    Monitor every inventory change over time and stay in full control of stock movements.
  • Inventory valuation
    View the report to understand inventory costs and assess potential profits and margins.
  • Label printing
    Print barcode labels to add items faster to sales, purchase orders and inventory counts.
