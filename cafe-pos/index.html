Transform your Café business with Loyverse POS System

Easy to get started

Get started quickly with helpful guides and 24/7 Loyverse support.

Get Started

Integrated payments

Loyverse POS app integrates with the best payment providers around the world, using which you can simplify the sales process for your customers.

Integrated payments

Scale your business

Manage one or multiple cafés under a single account, track inventory, employees, and customers, and view sales analytics.

Multi Store

Smooth order management

  • Baristas can easily receive and manage orders with Open tickets.
  • Assign orders to tables in one click using predefined tickets.
  • Manage open tickets by splitting, merging, and moving items between them.
  • Assign tickets to different employees.

Manage your employees

  • Track each employee’s sales performance and make informed business decisions.
  • See busy hours and manage staff more efficiently.
  • Manage employee access to sensitive information and functions.
  • Employees can clock in/out and the system calculates their total hours.
  • Identify any cash discrepancy at the end of the shift and track employees’ inaccuracy or manipulations.

Explore Employee management

Organize sales according to your needs

  • Customize your menu, bring popular items to the first screen.
  • Organize items and categories on separate pages, such as coffee, cakes, and fruits.
  • Add toppings and add-ons to items in one click with modifiers.
  • Create dining options such as “Dine in,” “Take out,” and “Delivery.”
  • Easily apply discounts and taxes, and add notes to items or orders.

Make your customers come back

  • Encourage repeat visits by rewarding customers with bonus points through a built-in loyalty program.
  • Turn first-time visitors into regulars by rewarding every purchase.
  • Track customer preferences with notes to provide a personalized experience.

Explore the Loyalty program

Manage your inventory

  • Quickly restock inventory using purchase orders.
  • Manage stock of produced items and ingredients using composite items.
  • Transfer inventory between café locations.
  • Keep stock accurate with inventory counts and stock adjustments in Advanced Inventory.

Explore Advanced Inventory

Connect your favorite business tools

Automate your workflows by connecting Loyverse with integrations for accounting, e-commerce, and marketing.
