Smarter way to accept card payments

Unlock fast and seamless transactions with Loyverse POS and Shift4, a trusted payment provider in New Zealand and Australia.

* Currently available only on iOS devices.

Create Shift4 account

Benefits of Loyverse & Shift4 integration

  • Eliminate manual input and reduce errors by automatically transferring purchase amounts from Loyverse POS to Shift4 EFTPOS, streamlining your payment process
  • Get started quickly with easy steps and start accepting payments right away
  • Monitor Shift4 transactions in the Loyverse Back Office for instant insights into your business performance

Why Shift4?

  • Portable EFTPOS machine with a long-lasting battery provides the flexibility to accept payments both at the counter and on the go
  • Accept card payments without transaction fees or terminal rental—Shift4’s automated surcharging covers the costs for you
  • Switch seamlessly between Wi-Fi, 4G, or Broadband when needed, so you never miss a sale
  • Stay supported around the clock to ensure everything runs smoothly at all times
  • All payments are encrypted for enhanced security, safeguarding both you and your customers throughout each transaction

Accept all the popular payment methods

  • Accept all major payment methods, including Visa, Mastercard, EFTPOS Debit, American Express, and more
  • Enable fast, contactless payments with Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay, Alipay and WeChat

Get started in three simple steps

1. Download the Loyverse POS app

Install the Loyverse POS app on your iPhone or iPad

2. Set up your Shift4 account

Sign up for Shift4 account and receive EFTPOS machine

3. Connect Loyverse to EFTPOS machine

Follow the easy setup instructions and start accepting payments
