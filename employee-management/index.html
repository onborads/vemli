Employee Management

Smart tools for managing your staff

See team performance, manage permissions, and track hours worked — all in one place.

Get started Chat with us

Understand and improve your team’s performance

See your team’s performance clearly to make better decisions and support your staff

  • Track sales by employee to identify top performers and where support is needed
  • Know when your store is busiest to plan better staffing
  • Automatically assign tips from integrated card payments to the employee who served the customer for clear and fair tracking

Control employee access

Set the right access for your team to protect the business and keep operations under control

  • Create roles with different access levels
  • Give each employee PIN access to the POS app for secure and quick login
  • Restrict access to sensitive information and actions like discounts, refunds, or voiding sales

Track employee working hours

Get a clear overview of your team’s hours to manage labor costs and keep payroll accurate

  • Automatically track hours with PIN-based clock-in and clock-out
  • Review and adjust time cards to see total hours worked for each employee
  • Keep accurate attendance records to simplify payroll and control labor costs
