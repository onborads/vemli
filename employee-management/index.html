Understand and improve your team’s performance

See your team’s performance clearly to make better decisions and support your staff

Control employee access

Frequently asked questions

Can employees access both POS and Back Office?

You can choose whether employees can access the POS app, the Back Office, or both, based on their assigned role.

Can I manage employees for multiple stores from one account?

Yes. If you use multiple stores in Loyverse, you can manage staff access and review employee data for each store from the same Back Office account.

Is there a limit to how many employees I can add?

No. You can add as many employees as you need and manage access by role as your team grows.
