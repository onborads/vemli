The Ultimate POS System for your bar and pub

Intuitive, flexible and easy to use point of sale for bar and pub.

Speed and Efficiency

Vemli POS app can handle a large number of visitors thanks to fast order entry. It can provide fast service and reduce waiting time, which is important for a busy bar.

Vemli POS

Connect integrations

Increase your bar efficiency by connecting accounting, ordering, marketing, and digital receipt applications, to provide seamless operations, and streamlined management.

App Marketplace

Scale your business

Manage multiple individual bars or an entire bar chain under one account, track inventory, employees, and customers, and analyze sales data.

Multi Store

Sell composite drinks

  • Take orders in just one click with predefined sets of volumes (for example shots or pints) for your drinks.
  • Create items, such as cocktails consisting with other drinks by using composite items functionality.
  • Track inventory and low stock of the components of your drinks.

Smooth order management

  • Bartenders can take orders on an Android or iOS devices.
  • Quickly and accurately receive many orders with Open tickets.
  • Manage open tickets by splitting, merging, and moving items between tickets.
  • Assign tickets to different employees.
  • If you have kitchen, you can send orders there by using kitchen displays or printers.

Enhance the loyalty and customer service in your bar

  • Display order information to your customers on a separate digital display simultaneously with the sale process.
  • Motivate customers to visit your bar or pub again by rewarding them with bonus points using the loyalty program.
  • Create a pool of constant visitors of your bar.
  • Make notes about customers to remember each client's personal preferences and to provide the best service.

Explore the Loyalty program

Manage your employees

  • Track each employee’s sales performance and make informed business decisions.
  • Information on the workload per hour allows for efficient management of your staff.
  • Manage employee access to sensitive information and functions.
  • Employees can clock in/out and the system calculates their total hours working in the bar.
  • Identify any cash discrepancy at the end of the shift and track employees’ inaccuracy or manipulations.

Explore Employee management

Customize sales screen for your needs

  • Customize the menu screen according to your needs; organize items and categories by different types. For example, drinks, wine, cocktails etc.
  • Easily manage add-ons to the drinks by using modifiers.
  • Apply discounts and taxes, and make notes to the items or to the orders.

Manage your inventory

  • Quickly fill up your stock with fresh products with purchase orders.
  • Manage stock of produced items and ingredients with the production of composite items.
  • Transfer goods between your pubs.
  • Keep your stock always accurate by performing counting and stock correction in the Advanced Inventory Management.

Explore Advanced Inventory

Streamline your bar operations with integrations

Optimize your bar business workflow by connecting your Vemli account with various integrations: WhatsApp receipts, online ordering, marketing.
