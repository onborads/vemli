Frequently asked questions

What is a loyalty program?

A loyalty program rewards customers for staying connected to your store. Each time they make a purchase, they earn points that can be used for future discounts. It's a simple way to build stronger relationships and encourage repeat visits.

I'm already using another loyalty program. Will I lose all my existing customer data if I switch to Loyverse?

Not at all. You can import your existing customer data into Loyverse using a CSV file, including names, contact information, and even current point balances. This makes switching smooth and hassle-free.

Do customers need a physical loyalty card to join the program?

No, customers can be added using their name, phone number, or barcode-based digital loyalty card, whatever works best for your workflow.
