Seamless card payments with Loyverse and Tyro

Transform your payment experience with Loyverse POS and Tyro, a leading payment provider in Australia.

* Currently available only on iOS devices.

create tyro account

Advantages of Loyverse and Tyro integration

  • Minimize manual input and errors by automatically transferring purchase amounts from Loyverse POS to Tyro terminal, simplifying your payment processing
  • Process payments anywhere using Tyro’s mobile terminals integrated with Loyverse POS—designed for fast and flexible transactions wherever you are
  • Track your business performance in real-time, enabling better decision-making and forecasting through data automatically recorded in the Loyverse Back Office

Accept all major payment methods

Tyro EFTPOS machines can accept the following payment types: Visa Credit and Visa Debit, Mastercard® and Debit Mastercard®, eftpos, American Express - OptBlue, UnionPay, Alipay, Apple Pay, Google Pay™, and Samsung Pay.

Get more benefits with Tyro bank account

  • No batch, account-keeping, or dishonor fees with a Tyro Bank Account
  • High interest on term deposits and daily interest for funds left in your Tyro Bank Account
  • Receive same-day settlements when depositing into your Tyro Bank Account
  • Tyro is an Authorized Deposit-taking Institution with government-backed deposit guarantees
  • Pay anyone directly using BPAY® or BSB for one-time or recurring payments, enhancing your payment efficiency
