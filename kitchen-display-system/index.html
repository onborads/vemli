Frequently asked questions

What is Loyverse Kitchen Display System?

Loyverse KDS is a Kitchen Display System for iPad and Android tablets that shows orders sent from Loyverse POS. It gives kitchen staff a clear view of every order and streamlines meal preparation. By replacing kitchen printers and paper tickets, it helps you work faster and reduce paper waste.

What information does KDS display?

KDS shows all essential order details: item names, quantities, modifiers, and comments. Tickets are also color-coded to indicate how much time has passed since the order was placed, making it easier to prioritize dishes.

Can KDS handle dietary restrictions or special requests?

Yes. Notes entered in the POS, such as allergies or special instructions, are displayed on KDS so your staff can prepare dishes accurately.

How much does Loyverse KDS cost?

Loyverse KDS is completely free. You can download it from the Google Play Store or the App Store.

What hardware is Loyverse KDS compatible with?

Loyverse KDS works on iPad (iOS 15.0 or higher) and Android tablets (Android 7.0 or higher). While the minimum requirement is a 7" screen, we recommend at least a 10" display for better visibility. Tablets should be positioned in landscape mode. You can also connect an iOS POS device to an Android KDS, and vice versa.

How do I set up Loyverse KDS?

First, create a Loyverse account and install at least one Loyverse POS app. Then, install the Loyverse KDS app on a separate tablet and place it in the kitchen (landscape mode recommended). POS and KDS connect via Wi-Fi through your router. Follow the setup guide to connect and configure.

Can I connect multiple KDS stations to the same POS?

Yes. You can connect multiple KDS stations to one POS. Each KDS can be configured to display orders from different item groups (e.g., kitchen, bar, desserts). These settings are managed in the Back Office and on the KDS app.
