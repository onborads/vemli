Streamlined kitchen order management

See all orders at a glance, with items, quantities, modifiers, and notes clearly displayed to keep preparation on track and reduce mistakes.

Headers change color to indicate waiting time, so staff can prioritize easily.

Frequently asked questions

What is Vemli Kitchen Display System?

Vemli KDS is a Kitchen Display System for iPad and Android tablets that shows orders sent from Vemli POS. It gives kitchen staff a clear view of every order and streamlines meal preparation. By replacing kitchen printers and paper tickets, it helps you work faster and reduce paper waste.

What information does KDS display?

KDS shows all essential order details: item names, quantities, modifiers, and comments. Tickets are also color-coded to indicate how much time has passed since the order was placed, making it easier to prioritize dishes.

Can KDS handle dietary restrictions or special requests?

Yes. Notes entered in the POS, such as allergies or special instructions, are displayed on KDS so your staff can prepare dishes accurately.

What hardware is Vemli KDS compatible with?

Vemli KDS works on iPad (iOS 15.0 or higher) and Android tablets (Android 7.0 or higher). While the minimum requirement is a 7" screen, we recommend at least a 10" display for better visibility. Tablets should be positioned in landscape mode. You can also connect an iOS POS device to an Android KDS, and vice versa.

Can I connect multiple KDS stations to the same POS?

Yes. You can connect multiple KDS stations to one POS. Each KDS can be configured to display orders from different item groups (e.g., kitchen, bar, desserts). These settings are managed in the Back Office and on the KDS app.
