Frequently asked questions

Can Vemli POS process card payments?

While Vemli POS doesn't directly process card payments, it seamlessly integrates with leading payment providers, allowing you to accept card payments easily and securely through supported card readers.

I already have a payment provider — do I have to switch?

Vemli partners with top-tier payment providers to offer you the best rates and solutions for your business. If your provider isn’t integrated with Vemli, you can still accept non-integrated card payments with Vemli POS. You’ll just need to manually enter the sales totals on your card reader, because payments won’t automatically sync between Vemli POS and the card reader. For more details, see How to Accept Credit Card Payments.

Will it take lots of time to setup integrated payments?

We’ve worked with our payment partners to create a seamless solution — including making the setup process as quick and easy as possible. Our help articles also offer detailed guides to assist you every step of the way.

Can I use several payment providers with Vemli?

Yes, you can use multiple payment providers with Vemli. Set them up as different payment types and select the preferred option at checkout.
