About Us

Loyverse is a suite of POS (point-of-sale) mobile applications. Our tools help merchants around the world to manage their sales, inventories, employees, and customers.

The name stands for the Loyalty Universe. It reflects our conviction that customer loyalty is an essential factor for businesses to be successful.

Our customers love Loyverse mobile apps for its straightforward to learn and simple to use interface. We believe that by empowering small businesses, which is the backbone of every country’s economy, we contribute to the welfare of humanity.

Who we are?

Our team has a foundation of 20+ years of experience in the field of point of sale software development and integrations with payment gateways.

Loyverse started in 2014.

Loyverse products are available on both the Android and the iOS in 25+ languages and recognized in 170+ countries.
