Clothing store inventory

  • Track your stock and keep all your products ready to sell to your customer.
  • Use real-time sales data analysis to make informed inventory purchases.
  • Distribute goods to your chain stores with the transfer orders.
  • Keep all the data of your stock up to date with the inventory recount functionality.
  • Identify any cash discrepancy at the end of the shift and track employees’ inaccuracy or manipulations.
