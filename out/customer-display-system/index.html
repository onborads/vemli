Frequently asked questions

Is there an extra cost for Customer Display?

No. It’s free to download and included with every Loyverse POS account.

What hardware is Loyverse Customer Display compatible with?

The app works on iOS devices (iOS 15.0 or higher recommended) and Android devices (7.0 or higher). You can connect your POS running on iOS to a CDS on Android, and vice versa.

How do I set up Loyverse Customer Display?

First, create a Loyverse account and install the Loyverse POS app. Then install the CDS app on a separate tablet or mobile device facing your customers. Connect both devices to the same Wi-Fi network and link them in the POS settings. Follow these easy steps to connect and configure the customer display system.

Does it work without an internet connection?

Yes, Customer Display works offline as long as both POS and CDS are connected to the same local Wi-Fi network.

Do I need Loyverse POS to run the Customer Display?

Yes, the CDS app works exclusively with the Loyverse POS system.
