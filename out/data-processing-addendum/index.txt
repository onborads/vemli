                  <h1 itemprop="name">Data Processing Addendum</h1>

                  <p>
                    This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") is effective
                    &nbsp;upon the earlier of your clicking “Accept” to the
                    Terms of Use (the ‘Terms’) or your use of any of the
                    Services and forms part of the Terms between Loyverse
                    Commerce Ltd ("Loyverse Commerce") and the entity entering
                    the Terms as a user of the Services ("Customer").&nbsp;<br />
                    <br />
                    This DPA is supplemental to the Terms and sets out the roles
                    and obligations that apply when Loyverse Commerce processes
                    Personal Data falling within the scope of the GDPR on behalf
                    of Customer in the course of providing the Services.<br />
                    <br />
                    All capitalized terms not defined in this DPA shall have the
                    meanings set forth in the Terms.<br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>1. Definitions</strong><br />
                    1.1 For the purposes of this DPA:<br />
                    (a) "EEA" means the European Economic Area.<br />
                    (b) "GDPR" means Regulation 2016/679 of the European
                    Parliament and of the Council on<br />
                    the protection of natural persons with regard to the
                    processing of personal data and on the free movement of such
                    data, and repealing Directive 95/46/EC.<br />
                    (c) "Terms" means the terms of use or other written or
                    electronic agreement between Loyverse Commerce and Customer
                    setting out the provision and use of the Loyverse
                    Services.<br />
                    (d) The terms "Controller", "Processor", "Personal Data",
                    "processing", "special categories of data" and "Data
                    Subject" have the meanings given to them in the GDPR.<br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>2. Applicability of DPA</strong><br />
                    2.1 Applicability. This DPA will apply onwards to the extent
                    that Loyverse Commerce processes Personal Data falling
                    within the scope of the GDPR on behalf of Customer in the
                    course of providing the Services.<br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>3. Roles and Responsibilities</strong><br />
                    3.1 Roles of the Parties. As between Loyverse Commerce and
                    Customer, Customer is the Data Controller of the Personal
                    Data described in Annex A and Loyverse Commerce shall
                    process the Personal Data as a Data Processor acting on
                    behalf of Customer.<br />
                    <br />
                    3.2 Customer Processing of Personal Data. Customer shall be
                    responsible for:<br />
                    (a) Complying with all applicable laws relating to privacy
                    and data protection in respect of its use of the Services,
                    its processing of the Personal Data, and any processing
                    instructions it issues to Loyverse Commerce;<br />
                    (b) Ensuring it has the right to transfer, or provide access
                    to, the Personal Data to Loyverse Commerce for processing
                    pursuant to the Terms and this DPA; and<br />
                    <br />
                    3.3 Loyverse Commerce Processing of Personal Data. Loyverse
                    Commerce shall process the Personal Data for the purposes of
                    providing set out in Annex A and in accordance with the
                    lawful, documented instructions of Customer (including the
                    instructions of any users accessing the Loyverse Services on
                    Customer's behalf) as set out in the Terms, this DPA or
                    otherwise in writing.<br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>4. Security</strong><br />
                    4.1 Security. Loyverse Commerce shall implement appropriate
                    technical and organisational measures to protect the
                    Personal Data from accidental or unlawful destruction, loss,
                    alteration, unauthorised disclosure or access (a "Security
                    Incident").<br />
                    <br />
                    4.2 Confidentiality obligations. Loyverse Commerce shall
                    ensure that any personnel that it authorizes to process the
                    Personal Data shall be subject to a duty of
                    confidentiality.<br />
                    <br />
                    4.3 Security Incidents. Upon becoming aware of a Security
                    Incident affecting Personal Data processed by Loyverse
                    Commerce, Loyverse Commerce shall notify Customer without
                    undue delay. Loyverse Commerce shall make reasonable efforts
                    to identify the cause of the Security Incident and to take
                    such steps as Loyverse Commerce deems necessary and
                    reasonable to mitigate the effects of such Security
                    Incident, to the extent such efforts are within Loyverse
                    Commerce reasonable control. Loyverse Commerce shall make
                    reasonable efforts to provide such information as Customer
                    may reasonably require to enable Customer to fulfil any data
                    breach reporting obligations under the GDPR.<br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>5. Sub-processing</strong><br />
                    5.1 Sub-processors. Customer agrees that Loyverse Commerce
                    may engage Loyverse Commerce affiliates and third party
                    sub-processors (collectively, "Sub-processors") to process
                    Personal Data on Loyverse Commerce behalf provided that:<br />
                    (a) Loyverse Commerce shall maintain an up to date list of
                    Sub-processors at
                    <a href="https://loyverse.com/terms-use"
                      >https://loyverse.com/terms-use</a
                    >
                    which it shall update with details of any change in
                    Sub-processors at least ten 10 days prior to any such
                    change;<br />
                    (b) Loyverse Commerce imposes on such Sub-processors data
                    protection terms that require it to protect the Personal
                    Data to the standard required by applicable data protection
                    laws; and<br />
                    (c) Loyverse Commerce remains liable for any breach of the
                    DPA caused by a Sub-processor.
                  </p>

                  <p>
                    5.2 Objection to Sub-processors. Customer may object to
                    Loyverse Commerce appointment or replacement of a
                    Sub-processor prior to its appointment or replacement
                    provided such objection is based on reasonable grounds
                    relating to data protection. In such event, the parties
                    shall cooperate in good faith to reach a resolution and if
                    such resolution cannot be reached, then Loyverse Commerce,
                    at its discretion, will either not appoint or replace the
                    Sub-processor or, will permit Customer to suspend or
                    terminate the affected Service (without prejudice to any
                    fees incurred by Customer prior to suspension or
                    termination).<br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>6. International Transfers</strong><br />
                    6.1 International transfers. To the extent that Loyverse
                    Commerce transfers any Personal Data originating from the
                    EEA to a country that has not been designated by the
                    European Commission as providing an adequate level of data
                    protection, it shall put in place such measures as are
                    necessary to ensure such transfer is in compliance with the
                    GDPR. Customer authorizes transfers of Personal Data to such
                    destinations outside of the EEA subject to such appropriate
                    safeguards having been put in place.<br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>7. Cooperation</strong><br />
                    7.1 Data subject rights. Loyverse Commerce shall, taking
                    into account the nature of the processing, provide
                    reasonable assistance to Customer insofar as this is
                    possible, to enable Customer to respond to requests from
                    data subjects seeking to exercise their rights under the
                    GDPR. In the event such request is made directly to Loyverse
                    Commerce, Loyverse Commerce shall promptly inform Customer
                    of the same.<br />
                    <br />
                    7.2 Data protection impact assessments. Loyverse Commerce
                    shall, taking into account the nature of the processing and
                    the information available to it, provide reasonable
                    assistance needed to fulfil Customer's obligation to carry
                    out data protection impact assessments and prior
                    consultations with supervisory authorities, to the extent
                    required under the GDPR and to the extent Customer does not
                    otherwise have access to the relevant information.<br />
                    <br />
                    7.3 Provision of information and reports. Loyverse Commerce
                    shall make available to the controller all information
                    necessary to demonstrate compliance with the obligations
                    laid down in this DPA by request to
                    <a href="mailto:privacy@loyverse.com"
                      >privacy@loyverse.com</a
                    >.<br />
                    <br />
                    7.4. Audit. Whilst it is the parties' intention ordinarily
                    to rely on the provision of the documentation to verify
                    Loyverse Commerce compliance with this DPA, Loyverse
                    Commerce shall permit the Customer (or its appointed third
                    party auditors) to carry out an audit of Loyverse Commerce
                    processing of Personal Data under the Terms following a
                    Security Incident suffered by Loyverse Commerce, or upon the
                    instruction of a data protection authority. Customer must
                    give Loyverse Commerce reasonable prior notice of such
                    intention to audit, conduct its audit during normal business
                    hours, and take all reasonable measures to prevent
                    unnecessary disruption to Loyverse Commerce operations. Any
                    such audit shall be subject to Loyverse Commerce security
                    and confidentiality terms and guidelines. If Loyverse
                    Commerce declines to follow any instruction requested by
                    Customer regarding audits, Customer is entitled to terminate
                    this DPA and the Terms.<br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>8. Return/Deletion of Data</strong><br />
                    8.1 Return or deletion of Personal Data. Upon termination or
                    expiry of the Terms, Loyverse Commerce shall delete or
                    return to Customer the Personal Data (including copies) in
                    Loyverse Commerce possession in accordance with the
                    procedures and timeframes specified in the Terms. This
                    requirement shall not apply to the extent that Loyverse
                    Commerce is required by applicable law to retain some or all
                    of the Personal Data. For Personal Data archived on backup
                    systems, Loyverse Commerce shall delete this data generally
                    within 6 months of termination or expiry of the Terms (where
                    reasonably possible).<br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>9. Miscellaneous</strong><br />
                    9.1 Except as amended by this DPA, the Terms will remain in
                    full force and effect.<br />
                    9.2 Any claims brought under this DPA shall be subject to
                    the Terms, including but not limited to the exclusions and
                    limitations of liability set forth in the Terms.<br />
                    9.3 If there is a conflict between this DPA and the Terms,
                    the DPA will control.
                  </p>
