                            <h3 class="question" itemprop="name">
                              Does Loyverse POS work offline?
                              <i class="arrow"></i>
                            </h3>

                            <div
                              class="answer"
                              itemprop="acceptedAnswer"
                              itemscope=""
                              itemtype="https://schema.org/Answer"
                            >
                              <div itemprop="text">
                                <b>The Loyverse POS app works offline.</b> Your
                                device can continue to make sales and work with
                                shifts even when it is not connected to the
                                Internet.<br />
                                However, some features such as refunds, new
                                customer registration, adding items, and a few
                                other features will be unavailable or restricted
                                while offline.<br />
                                Check details in the help center:
                                <a
                                  href="https://help.loyverse.com/help/offline-work-of-pos"
                                  target="_blank"
                                  rel="noopener"
                                  >Offline Use of Loyverse POS</a
                                >
                              </div>
                            </div>
