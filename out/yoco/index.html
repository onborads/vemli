Get Started

Card payments made easy with Loyverse and Yoco

Streamline your payment experience with Loyverse POS seamlessly integrated with Yoco, a leading payment provider in South Africa.

* Currently available only on iOS devices.

create yoco account

Benefits of Loyverse and Yoco integration

  • Connect Yoco with Loyverse POS in minutes
  • Increase the speed and accuracy at checkout with Loyverse and Yoco integrated payments, as transaction data is automatically synced with your card machine
  • Monitor transactions processed via Yoco directly from the Loyverse Back Office
  • Take advantage of fast fraud detection and reliable customer support, ensuring every transaction is secure and seamless

Why choose Yoco?

  • No rental fees or settlement costs
  • Low transaction rates for card payments
  • Portable and easy-to-use card machines
  • Trusted by businesses across various industries

Flexible payment options

  • Accept a wide range of payment options such as Visa, Mastercard, Amex, SASSA cards, Google Pay, Apple Pay, Samsung Pay
  • Offer PIN, contactless, and swipe & sign payment options to suit your customers' preferences

Get started with Loyverse and Yoco

1. Create a Loyverse account

Sign up for your free Loyverse account

2. Download the Loyverse POS app

Install the Loyverse POS app on your iOS device

3. Connect Yoco to Loyverse

Sign up for Yoco account and receive your card reader. Follow step-by-step guide to connect your Yoco card reader with the Loyverse POS.
