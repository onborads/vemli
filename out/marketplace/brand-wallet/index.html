About

BrandWallet is not an APP! It places your brand's loyalty card in Apple Wallet and Google Wallet right next to your customers' credit cards! BrandWallet provides point based digital loyalty solutions for small businesses (Coffee shops, restaurants, car wash centers, beauty salons, clinics etc.) through “MOBILE WALLET” platforms. Compatible with Apple Wallet and Google Wallet.

BrandWallet is NOT an application, that's why it is a new trend! BrandWallet and Loyverse integration enables you to offer Digital Point Offers (Earn or burn points) to your customers.

Join thousands of companies of all sizes using BrandWallet to boost customer loyalty, increase repeat purchases and drive higher spending to build a more profitable business.
