                        <h2>About</h2>

                        <p>
                          Waraqa (ورقة) is a digital receipt platform that helps
                          businesses replace paper receipts with secure digital
                          ones sent directly to customers.
                        </p>

                        <p>
                          When a purchase is made, the receipt can be
                          automatically delivered to the customer through the
                          Waraqa app using their phone number, allowing them to
                          easily access, organize, and track their purchases in
                          one place.
                        </p>

                        <p>
                          For businesses, Waraqa reduces paper costs, simplifies
                          receipt management, and unlocks powerful insights
                          about customer purchasing behavior.
                        </p>
                        <p></p>

                        <h3>Key benefits</h3>

                        <p>
                          For Businesses<br />
                          - Reduce paper receipt costs<br />
                          - Offer customers a modern digital receipt
                          experience<br />
                          - Access detailed analytics about purchases and
                          customer behavior<br />
                          - Enable targeted promotions to the right customers<br />
                          - More control of loyalty points managment and
                          configurations
                        </p>
                        <p></p>
                        <p>
                          For Customers<br />
                          - Store all receipts in one secure place<br />
                          - Easily track purchases and spending<br />
                          - Find receipts instantly for returns or warranties<br />
                          - Access loyalty rewards from participating stores
                        </p>
                        <p></p>

                        <h3>How to get started:</h3>
                        <p>
                          You can view all the details for how to setup and how
                          to use waraqa here
                          https://waraqareceipts.com/en/help/loyverse-integration
                        </p>

                        <p></p>
                        <h3>Data Flow:</h3>
                        <p>Receipts create/update: From loyverse to Waraqa</p>
                        <p>Loyalty points: from Waraqa to Loyverse</p>
                        <p></p>
