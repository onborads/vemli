                          <h2>About</h2>
                          <p>
                            Connect Loyverse with over 200+ trusted marketplaces
                            like Kogan, Temu, Amazon, Tiktok Shop, Noon, Shein,
                            Whatnot, Target Plus, Iconic, The Range, Backmarket,
                            Bandcamp, and more using Commercium. You can also
                            integrate Loyverse with leading e-commerce platforms
                            such as Shopify, WooCommerce, Wix, Bigcommerce,
                            Ecwid, Prestashop, Salla, and more, as well as OMS
                            tools (Zoho Inventory), ERP systems (Acumatica,
                            Business Central 365), and accounting tools like
                            QuickBooks. With Commercium, you can manage
                            products, orders, and inventory from a single
                            platform, eliminating the need for multiple costly
                            connectors.
                          </p>
                          <p></p>

                          <h3>Key benefits</h3>
                          <p>
                            - Reduce Cost: Eliminate the need for separate
                            connectors and manage your entire sales channel like
                            B&amp;Q, Galaxus, Wayfair, OnBuy, Farfetch, eBay,
                            Reverb, NOTHs operation from a single, intuitive
                            dashboard.<br />
                            - No Manual Efforts: Reduce errors and save time
                            with end-to-end automation.<br />
                            - Centralized Control: Sync Loyverse with all your
                            sales channels like CDiscount, Abebooks, Emag,
                            Faire, Vivino, Walmart in one place.
                          </p>
                          <p></p>

                          <h3>Data flow</h3>
                          <p>
                            - Orders from other sales channels like Refurbed,
                            Gunbroker, Groupon, Poshmark are automatically
                            created in Loyverse.<br />
                            - Inventory updates in Loyverse reflect instantly
                            across all sales channels like Zalora, Jumia Vendor,
                            Trademe, CDON, Namshi, and more.<br />
                            - Products created in Loyverse sync automatically to
                            all connected sales channels like CommerceHub, DSCO
                            more.
                          </p>
