NearSt

Boost sales by showing in-store inventory on platforms like Google and Facebook

CONNECT APP

About

An easy integration to connect your in-store inventory to platforms like Google and Facebook. Attract local shoppers that are looking online for the products you’ve got in stock.

Show your inventory where your customers are

You can make sure your inventory shows up when someone nearby searches for your products or store in:
Google Search
Google Maps
Google Shopping
Google Images
Google My Business (See What’s In Store)
Google Voice Assistant
Google Local Inventory Ads (Optional add-on)
Facebook Shops
Facebook Messenger
WhatsApp
Instagram [Launching soon]

And improve your offering

NearSt also includes unique local insights into what shoppers in your area are interested in - improving your offering based on local shopping needs.

Simple set-up.

No manual upload or e-commerce solution needed. NearSt automatically sources all stock data from Loyverse and ensures it gets to the sites and apps shoppers use.

Requirement - Good stock control of your inventory in Loyverse system

Not required - As an Loyverse customer the following is not required:
No need to have a website.
No need to have product profiles like images, titles or descriptions.
No setup or maintenance. Everything is set up for you, so you can focus on running your business.
