About

JomeInvoice is a Malaysia-born e-invoice middleware that gives small and medium-sized businesses an compliance layer for the LHDN e-Invoice regime: the platform is certified 100 % in line with the latest LHDN. JomeInvoice becomes a low-friction way for Malaysian merchants to meet the July 2025 mandate, cut manual workload and future-proof their invoicing processes in one move.

Key benefits

- No complex setup, fast onboarding and seamless integration with Loyverse
- Always up-to-date with Malaysia e-invoice compliance
- Live dashboards show submission status, invoice counts, SFTP/CSV upload logs and a full audit trail—all exportable for auditors.
- Easy monthly consolidate submission and cover self billing for your business
- FREE setup fee and e-invoice template for your business
- FREE request e-invoice portal by customers
- FREE readiness assessment, step-by-step onboarding, live and on-demand training, and Malaysia-based support.

How to get started

1. Click the ""Connect App"" button.
2. Log in Loyverse account and authorize Jomeinvoice to access your data.
3. Log in to the Jome account or sign up.
4. Select which workspace to install Loyverse in JomeInvoice.
4. Now you are ready!

Data Flow

Sales Invoice data sync from Loyverse to Jome whenever a new receipt is created in Loyverse.

1. Create a new receipt from the Loyverse POS.
2. Then, head over to the Sales > Invoice > Loyverse channel on Jomeinvoice, click on the `Refresh` button on the top right corner (it might take a while to sync the data from Loyverse).
3. Now, you should be able to see the receitp showing up in the JomeInvoice Loyverse channel
4. You have successfully connected to JomeInvoice.

Refund data sync from Loyverse to Jome whenever a new refund is created in Loyverse.

1. Create a new Refund from the Loyverse POS.
2. Then, head over to the Sales > Refund > Loyverse channel on Jomeinvoice, click on the `Refresh` button on the top right corner (it might take a while to sync the data from Loyverse).
3. Now, you should be able to see the refund showing up in the JomeInvoice Loyverse channel.
4. You have successfully created refund note to JomeInvoice.
