                          <h2>Loyverse Shopify Integration</h2>

                          <p>
                            <strong>Loyverse Shopify Integration</strong> is
                            user-friendly and easy to understand, whether you
                            sell a single item or 1 million items or more. Keep
                            track of stock movement between
                            <strong>Loyverse and Shopify</strong>. Our Platform
                            can streamline and automate the inventory and sales
                            with customers syncing. Loyverse Shopify integration
                            automatically update inventory to Shopify, saving
                            your time and reducing costly errors. Our automated
                            process will sync product catalog details from
                            Loyverse POS to your Shopify and sales orders from
                            Shopify to Loyverse POS. It will automatically
                            decimate inventory levels after-sales. When these
                            processes are not automated, mistakes may happen,
                            product details could be incorrect or missing and
                            increase the overselling on your online sales
                            channels.
                          </p>

                          <h3>
                            Key benefits of Loyverse and Shopify integration
                          </h3>

                          <ul>
                            <li>
                              Sync all or selected products from Loyverse to
                              Shopify
                            </li>
                            <li>
                              Accurate inventory syncing to avoid overselling
                            </li>
                            <li>
                              Simplified process to manage price and inventory
                              changes in products
                            </li>
                            <li>
                              Sync Shopify orders and customers data to Loyverse
                              as sales receipt
                            </li>
                            <li>Multi-location synchronization</li>
                            <li>Save time and reducing costly errors</li>
                            <li>No programming skills needed</li>
                            <li>No account setup fee</li>
                            <li>No long contract only monthly subscription</li>
                            <li>15 days free trial account</li>
                            <li>
                              Smooth and easy process of integration, you can
                              integrate your Loyverse and Shopify in few clicks.
                            </li>
                          </ul>
