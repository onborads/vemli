                        <h1>
                          A visionary entrepreneur runs a laundry cafe with
                          Loyverse POS
                        </h1>
                        <p>
                          LAUNDRAFÉ group runs various businesses, including
                          cutting-edge technology, and Loyverse supports it.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          The word "LAUNDRAFÉ" is a combination of Laundry and
                          <a href="https://loyverse.com/cafe-pos">cafe</a>,
                          where people can take a coffee and sweets while
                          waiting for laundry.
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          <strong
                            >- Could you explain about your business?</strong
                          >
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          When I visited Japan in May of 2018, I saw a laundry
                          cafe. The idea came to my mind. Because in my area,
                          there are a lot of dormitories. After returning to my
                          town, I started sending Emails to those dormitories
                          that I would like to open a laundry cafe. They were
                          interested in my plan and helped me to start this
                          cafe. This is how it has started.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          Currently, we have 4 services: self-service laundry,
                          cafe, smart lockers, and professional cleaning
                          service. This is also the idea that I got from Japan.
                          We also have a professional service called Dr.P, which
                          provides shoe fixing, pet cafe, cleaning, toy fixing,
                          bag care, fitting, and home cleaning.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/13-cafe.png"
                            alt="cafe"
                            width="800"
                            height="auto"
                          />
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/13-laundry-cafe.png"
                            alt="self-service laundry"
                            width="800"
                            height="auto"
                          />
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/13-smart-locker.png"
                            alt="smart lockers"
                            width="750"
                            height="auto"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          <strong
                            >- How do you get to benefit from Loyverse?</strong
                          >
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          There is a system for laundry to control the laundry
                          machines, and it is connected to Loyverse through
                          <a href="/loyverse-pos-api">API</a>. I like using
                          Loyverse because it has an intuitive interface, and I
                          can see sales analysis in the
                          <a href="https://loyverse.com/en/signup">BackOffice</a
                          >. That is why we are also using Loyverse for another
                          cafe and Dr.P as well. We also operate another account
                          for
                          <a href="/advanced-inventory">inventory management</a>
                          only.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <br /><img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/13-order-here.png"
                            alt="system for laundry to control the laundry machines"
                            width="800"
                            height="auto"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          <strong
                            >- May I know what is your vision for the
                            future?</strong
                          >
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          We want to expand our business more in the service
                          sector to provide more jobs to the people. This is the
                          main motivation for me. We want to help people as much
                          as possible. There are people and communities who are
                          in need. Also, I believe in Good Karma.
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          We currently have limited funding, so I'm interested
                          in receiving investment from Japan to expand the smart
                          locker business. We can connect smart lockers to our
                          existing businesses, food, delivery, logistics,
                          laundry, and we can expand into the fintech area as
                          well. Our service can be expanded to the entire
                          capital Manila.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"

                            width="318"
                            height="auto"
                          />
                        </p>

                          <em
                            >Elcid Lao, founder and CEO of LAUNDRAFÉ group.</em
