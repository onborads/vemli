                        <h1>
                          How to send Loyverse receipts to customers in a PDF
                          file
                        </h1>
                        <p>
                          Loyverse POS System provides sending receipts to
                          customers by email or printed ones. Despite this, a
                          quite convenient way for Loyverse users is to send
                          receipts to their customers in PDF file.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          This step-by-step tutorial will explain how it is
                          possible to send receipts in PDF format using
                          <a href="https://loyverse.com/marketplace">Loyverse</a
                          >,
                          <a href="https://www.make.com/en/?fromImt=1"
                            >Make tools</a
                          >, Google services, and the Twilio sending platform.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          Before starting, you should have a Loyverse account, a
                          Make account, a Google account, and a Twilio account.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          Login into Make and select the “<a
                            href="https://www.make.com/en/templates/8410-send-a-download-link-to-the-loyverse-receipt-in-pdf-by-sms-using-twilio?fromImt=1&amp;"
                            rel="noopener external nofollow"
                            target="_blank"
                            >Send a download link to the Loyverse receipt in PDF
                            by SMS using Twilio</a
                          >” template.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-01-login-integromat.png"
                            alt="Login into Make"
                            width="800"
                            height="434"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          Create a scenario by clicking on the “Create new
                          scenario from template” button.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-02-create-scenario.png"
                            alt="Create new scenario from template"
                            width="800"
                            height="450"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          In <strong>Step 1</strong> you have to set up
                          Webhooks. For that, first, click the “Add” button, set
                          a meaningful name, and press the ”Save” button.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-03-setup-webhooks.png"
                            alt="you have to set up Webhooks"
                            width="800"
                            height="423"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          In the next step, click the “Copy address to
                          clipboard” button.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-04-copy-address.png"
                            alt="Copy address to clipboard"
                            width="592"
                            height="565"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          Then go to the
                          <a
                            href="https://r.loyverse.com/dashboard/#/webhooks"
                            rel="noopener external nofollow"
                            target="_blank"
                            >Webhook section</a
                          >
                          of the Back office of your Loyverse account. Press the
                          “+Add Webhook” button.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-05-loyverse-add-webhook.png"
                            alt="Add Webhook"
                            width="621"
                            height="449"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          Select “Receipt created or updated” from the event
                          list.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-06-receipt-create.png"
                            alt="Receipt created or updated"
                            width="621"
                            height="524"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          Paste previously copied address from the Make Webhook
                          module. Press the “Save” button.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-07-paste-address.png"
                            alt="copied address from the Make Webhook module"
                            width="525"
                            height="443"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          In <strong>Step 2</strong> proceed with the “Continue”
                          button for taking into account only Sales receipts.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-08-step2-continue.png"
                            alt="Continue button"
                            width="620"
                            height="362"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          In <strong>Step 3</strong>, you have to add a Loyverse
                          connection to your scenario. Select the deprecated
                          connection type, give a meaningful name to the
                          connection, and insert the Access token (See:
                          <a
                            href="https://help.loyverse.com/help/loyverse-api"
                            rel="noopener external nofollow"
                            target="_blank"
                            >How to create tokens for API</a
                          >) of your Loyverse account.
                        </p>
                        <p>Then, press the “Continue” button.</p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-09-add-loyverse-connection.png"
                            alt="add a Loyverse connection to your scenario"
                            width="800"
                            height="417"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          Press the “Continue” button once again to save your
                          Loyverse connection.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-10-save-loyverse-connection.png"
                            alt="save your Loyverse connection"
                            width="660"
                            height="417"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          On <strong>Step 4</strong> and
                          <strong>Step 5</strong> proceed with the same Loyverse
                          connection which you used on <strong>Step 3</strong>.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-11-step4.png"
                            alt="Step 4 and 5 same Loyverse connection"
                            width="618"
                            height="446"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          In <strong>Steps 6</strong> to
                          <strong>16</strong> proceed with the “Continue”
                          button.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-12-step6.png"
                            alt="Continue button"
                            width="460"
                            height="246"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          In <strong>Step 17</strong>, Add your Google account
                          connection.&nbsp;<br />Click “Add”, then the
                          “Continue” button.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-13-add-google-account.png"
                            alt="Add your Google account connection"
                            width="750"
                            height="297"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          After choosing your <strong>Google account</strong>,
                          click on the ”Allow” button to give Integomat access
                          to your Google account.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-14-allow-integromat-access.png"
                            alt="Allow Integomat access"
                            width="384"
                            height="550"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          Choose a place where you want to save your document
                          and press the “Continue” button.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-15-choose-place.png"
                            alt="Choose a place to save document"
                            width="419"
                            height="657"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          <strong>Note:</strong> The folder chosen for New
                          Document’s Location should be open for access for
                          those who have a link.&nbsp;<br />To do so, open your
                          Google Drive, find the selected folder, press the down
                          arrow next to the folder name and click “Share.”
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-16-share.png"
                            alt="click Share"
                            width="470"
                            height="583"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          In the next window, click on “Change to anyone with
                          the link”.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-17-change-anyone.png"
                            alt="Change to anyone with the link"
                            width="662"
                            height="208"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>After that, click “Done” button to save changes.</p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-18-save-changes.png"
                            alt="save changes"
                            width="662"
                            height="383"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          In <strong>Step 18</strong> you should set up a Twilio
                          connection.&nbsp;<br />First, copy SID and Auth Token
                          from your Twilio account.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-19-copy-sid-auth-token.png"
                            alt="copy SID and Auth Token from Twilio account"
                            width="767"
                            height="643"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          Then click “Add” button, enter a meaningful connection
                          name and paste copied SID and Auth Token. Press
                          “Continue” button to save the connection.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-20-paste-token.png"
                            alt="paste copied SID and Auth Token"
                            width="749"
                            height="605"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          Press the “Continue” button once again to save the
                          Twilio module.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          After all previous steps, the whole scenario is set.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          Don’t forget to save your scenario by clicking the
                          “Disk” button.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-21-save-scenario.png"
                            alt="save your scenario"
                            width="601"
                            height="324"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          Move a slider to “On” and choose Scheduling settings
                          to run the scenario.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-22-scheduling-settings.png"
                            alt="choose Scheduling settings"
                            width="750"
                            height="375"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          Then, when a Loyverse user makes a sale in the POS and
                          the SALE receipt is created, a download PDF link for
                          this receipt will be sent to the customer attached to
                          SMS.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <strong>Note:</strong> To be able to send SMS you
                          should add your customers’ phone numbers to the
                          Loyverse Loyalty program in an
                          <a
                            href="https://www.twilio.com/docs/glossary/what-e164"
                            rel="noopener external nofollow"
                            target="_blank"
                            >E.164 format</a
                          >.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-23-send-sms.png"
                            alt="send SMS E.164 format"
                            width="384"
                            height="204"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>
                          By clicking the link from SMS, customers will
                          automatically download a PDF file with receipt details
                          to their device.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          The name of the PDF file will be the same as the
                          receipt number.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                          <img
                            class="img-in-blog"
                            src="/sites/all/themes/loyversecom/images/blog/30-24-receipt-pdf-file.png"
                            alt="the receipt PDF file"
                            width="344"
                            height="595"
                          />
                        </p>
                        <p>&nbsp;</p>
                        <p>Enjoy using Loyverse Integration!</p>
