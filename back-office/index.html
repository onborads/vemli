Loyverse back office

Streamline your business operations

Loyverse Back Office provides comprehensive sales reports and tools to manage inventory, employees, and customers. It empowers you with valuable insights and enhanced control for the growth of your business.

get started chat with us
Sales analytics

Stay in control wherever you are with real-time reporting

  • Track sales trends and react promptly to changes
  • Determine top selling items and categories
  • View complete sales history
  • Browse reports on payment types, modifiers, discounts and taxes
  • Track expected cash amount at the end of each shift and identify discrepancies
  • Export sales data to the spreadsheets
Item management

Simplify item management and customization

  • Easily manage items with various options like size and color
  • Bulk import and export items using CSV files
  • Use modifiers to customize items with add-ons and special requests
  • Tailor discounts to your needs with options for fixed amounts or percentages
Stock control

Have the right products in stock at the right time

  • Track inventory in real time
  • Set stock levels and receive automatic low stock alerts
  • Send purchase orders to suppliers and monitor stock receipts
  • Perform full or partial inventory counts with a barcode scanner or manually
  • Gain insights into the flow of your inventory by viewing complete adjustment log
  • View report on the cost and potential profit of your inventory

Explore inventory management

Employee management

Efficiently manage your staff with better visibility and control

  • Track sales by employee to evaluate individual performance
  • Enable smooth time tracking for your team as they clock in and out, with automated calculation of their total work hours
  • Assign varying access levels to safeguard sensitive data

Explore employee management

Customer loyalty

Turn occasional visitors into loyal customers

  • Set loyalty program to reward customers for their recurring purchases
  • Analyze the number of visits and the customer purchases amount to identify your loyal customers
  • View customer purchase history to provide personalized service
  • Keep notes on valuable customer preferences

Explore loyalty program

Multi store management

Expand your business across multiple locations

  • Manage multiple stores and POS devices from a single account
  • Gain instant insights into the performance of all your stores
  • Easily oversee items, employees, and customers across multiple locations

Explore multi-store management
