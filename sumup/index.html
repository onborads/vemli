Simplify payments with Vemli POS and SumUp

Provide your customers with a smooth, flexible, and secure payment experience using SumUp card readers, seamlessly integrated with Vemli POS.

create sumup account

Advantages of using SumUp

SumUp is an electronic payment provider, recognized globally for empowering merchants with its point-of-sale solutions.

  • No monthly fixed costs. No rental. No contract
  • Low transaction rates for debit and credit cards
  • Easy, fast, and secure payments
  • Access fast and reliable customer support to assist with your needs

Fast setup, hassle-free checkout

  • Easily connect your SumUp card reader to Vemli POS via Bluetooth and start accepting payments right away
  • Speed up checkout and reduce human errors by eliminating the manual entry of transaction amounts on the card reader
  • Enable tips and let customers choose a fixed percentage or custom amount at checkout
  • Save time with fast charging, getting fully charged in an hour, and enjoy longer-lasting power for continuous use

Accept popular payments types

  • Accept a wide range of payment options, including Visa, Mastercard, Maestro, American Express, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Samsung Pay
  • Offer chip & PIN and contactless payment options
